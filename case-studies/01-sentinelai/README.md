# SentinelAI Compliance — AI Risk Management Case Study

## Overview

SentinelAI is a fictional AI-powered compliance platform 
helping regulated businesses automate their compliance monitoring.

This case study demonstrates a comprehensive risk assessment 
aligned with the CRISC 8th Edition framework.

## What's Included

| Deliverable | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Risk Register | 55 risks across 8 categories |
| Heat Maps | Inherent and residual risk visualizations |
| Risk Assessment | Likelihood, impact, and scoring |
| Control Analysis | Existing controls and effectiveness |
| Treatment Decisions | Mitigate, accept, transfer, avoid |

## Risk Categories

- Strategic (6 risks)
- Operational (6 risks)
- AI/Model (7 risks)
- Agentic AI (8 risks)
- Cybersecurity (8 risks)
- Regulatory (8 risks)
- Third-Party/Vendor (6 risks)
- Financial (6 risks)
